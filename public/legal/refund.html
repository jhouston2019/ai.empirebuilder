Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We stand behind the quality of our AI Empire Builder Blueprint course. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a full refund within 30 days of your purchase date.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must:

  • Request the refund within 30 days of your purchase date
  • Provide a valid reason for the refund request
  • Not have downloaded or shared course materials inappropriately
  • Complete our refund request form

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Email us at refunds@aiempirebuilder.pro
  2. Include your order number and email address used for purchase
  3. Provide a brief explanation of why you're requesting a refund
  4. We'll respond within 24 hours with next steps

Refund Processing

Once your refund request is approved:

  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days
  • You'll receive a confirmation email when processed
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Your course access will be revoked upon refund completion

What Happens After a Refund

After your refund is processed:

  • Your account will be deactivated
  • You'll lose access to all course materials
  • You'll be removed from our community
  • You may not re-enroll in the course

Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Refund requests made after 30 days
  • Refunds for inappropriate use of course materials
  • Refunds for technical issues resolved within 48 hours
  • Refunds for change of mind after 14 days

Technical Issues

If you're experiencing technical difficulties:

  • Contact our support team first at support@aiempirebuilder.pro
  • We'll work to resolve the issue within 48 hours
  • If we can't resolve the issue, a refund will be provided
  • Most technical issues are resolved quickly

Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with our refund decision:

  • You may appeal by emailing legal@aiempirebuilder.pro
  • Include all relevant documentation
  • We'll review your case within 5 business days
  • Our decision is final

Contact Information

For refund-related questions or requests:

  • Email: refunds@aiempirebuilder.pro
  • Support: support@aiempirebuilder.pro
  • Response time: Within 24 hours

Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this refund policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
