PROFIT Scoring System

Score Your Idea

Rate each element 1-5 points:

  • Problem: How painful is it? (1-5)
  • Reachable: How easy to reach users? (1-5)
  • Obvious: How clear is monetization? (1-5)
  • Fast: How quickly can you build? (1-5)
  • Iterative: How much can it grow? (1-5)
  • Traffic: How many channels available? (1-5)

Total Score Interpretation:

  • 25-30: Excellent opportunity - build immediately!
  • 20-24: Good opportunity - worth pursuing
  • 15-19: Okay opportunity - needs refinement
  • <15: Poor opportunity - keep looking
