MARKET SIZE CALCULATION

TAM, SAM, SOM Framework

Market Sizing Example: Invoice Generator

  • TAM (Total Addressable Market):
    15M small businesses globally × $30/month = $450M/month
  • SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market):
    1M English-speaking freelancers × $30/month = $30M/month
  • SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market):
    0.1% market share × $30M = $30K/month potential

TREND ANALYSIS

// Google Trends analysis
const trendAnalysis = {
    checkGrowth(keyword) {
        // Look for:
        // - Consistent upward trend (good)
        // - Seasonal patterns (plan accordingly)
        // - Geographic hotspots (target first)
        // - Related queries rising (opportunities)
        
        const signals = {
            excellent: 'Steady growth > 20% YoY',
            good: 'Stable with seasonal peaks',
            risky: 'Declining or flat',
            avoid: 'Rapid decline or fad pattern'
        };
        
        return signals;
    },
    
    compareTerms: [
        'invoice generator',
        'invoice maker',
        'invoice builder',
        'invoice creator'
    ]
};

Red Flags to Avoid

  • No search volume (<100 searches/month)
  • Declining trend for 12+ months
  • 10+ established competitors with millions in funding
  • Requires specialized knowledge you don't have
  • Legal/compliance complexity
  • No clear user persona

Lesson 2.3: The 48-Hour Validation Sprint

From Idea to Validated Concept in 2 Days

Stop overthinking. Start validating. This sprint will tell you if your idea has legs.

HOUR-BY-HOUR SPRINT PLAN

Day 1: Research & Build (Hours 1-24)

Hours 1-4: Deep Research

  • Keyword research (volume, competition)
  • Find 10 competitors
  • Read 50+ forum posts about the problem
  • Document user language and pain points

Hours 5-8: Landing Page Creation

  • Choose domain ($10 or use subdomain)
  • Create landing page (Carrd, Webflow, or HTML)
  • Write compelling copy
  • Add email capture form

Hours 9-12: Mock-up Creation

  • Design tool interface (Figma, Canva)
  • Create 3-5 screenshots
  • Make demo GIF/video
  • Add to landing page

Hours 13-16: Initial Traffic

  • Share in 3 relevant subreddits
  • Post in 5 Facebook groups
  • Tweet with relevant hashtags
  • Message 10 potential users directly

Day 2: Validate & Decide (Hours 25-48)

Hours 25-28: Paid Traffic Test

  • Set up Google Ads ($50 budget)
  • Create 3 ad variations
  • Target high-intent keywords
  • Monitor cost per click

Hours 29-32: Direct Outreach

  • Email 20 potential customers
  • DM 20 people on Twitter/LinkedIn
  • Post in Slack communities
  • Run Twitter poll

Hours 33-40: Analyze Response

  • Check landing page conversion rate
  • Read all feedback
  • Calculate customer acquisition cost
  • Review competitor response

Hours 41-48: Go/No-Go Decision

  • Score against success metrics
  • Calculate potential revenue
  • Assess build complexity
  • Make final decision

VALIDATION METRICS

Success Criteria

Reddit Upvotes <10 10-50 >50

PRE-LAUNCH AUDIENCE BUILDING

Build Your Launch List

  • Content Marketing: Write 5 blog posts about the problem
  • Social Proof: Get 100 email signups before building
  • Community: Start a Discord/Slack for early users
  • Partnerships: Find 3 people to promote at launch
  • Press List: Identify 10 journalists who cover tools

Common Validation Mistakes

  • Asking friends/family (biased feedback)
  • Building before validating
  • Ignoring negative feedback
  • Validation in wrong channels
  • Not talking to actual users
  • Falling in love with your idea
